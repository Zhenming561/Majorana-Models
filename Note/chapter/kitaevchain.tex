\chapter{From Kitaev Chain to Topological Quantum Computation}

\section{Kitaev Chain}

A toy model which exhibits unpaired Majorana fermions. This catch two important properties:
\begin{itemize}
    \item The $U(1)$ symmetry $\hat{c}_j \mapsto \ee^{\ii \phi} \hat{c}_j$, corresponding to the electric charge conservation, must be broken down to a $\Z_2$ symmetry, $\oc_j \mapsto - \oc_j$
\end{itemize}
